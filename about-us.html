Prasann Kedia

CEO of the chill life - Prasann is the visionary behind Stok. His favorite question: What’s new? Constantly on his toes to push for innovation and strategic thinking. His long years in this industry and inspiring energy makes him pretty perfect for the role!

Vedant Kedia

Master storyteller, Marketing Head, and The Chiller of Chillers - Vedant is the bridge that connects us to other chillers. He’s the one who steers the brand’s tale and is spreading our laidback mission to jazz up life! Creative, passionate, and the epitome of easygoing; sometimes we wonder if Stok is actually him?? Tell us Vedant!

Anshuman Kedia

COO and gang leader - Anshuman is the groovy engine that keeps Stok’s rhythm running steady and smooth. He’s a born puzzle-cracker and an operational powerhouse, and boy we’re glad for that. The wind in our creative sails, he steers the ship to incredible horizons while having an absolute ball.

Nitin Tiberewal

Sales Chief and goal guru - everyone loves Nitin! He’s the king of customer service and has the most infectious energy - it’s practically impossible to not feel positive around his sunshine vibes. Naturally charismatic and always ready to dive in - his leadership is downright stellar and his numbers? Simply off the charts!

Tushar Bhandari

Business Development Head - Tushar is Stok’s favourite because he’s the one who gets our panda to new places, parties, and people! Analytical, competitive and on the pulse of the industry, he’s always ahead of the curve in identifying new business opportunities. All of this without breaking a sweat - how do you do it Tushar!!!??

S L Mittal

Production Head and Master Brewer - SL's technical know-how spans over 2 decades and chill leadership is smoother than our finest lager. He doesn’t just go the extra mile – he hops, skips, and jumps it, all in the pursuit of leading our teams to craft exceptional flavours. Cheers SL for always raising the bar!

G M Kamlesh

Assistant Production Head - Kamlesh is our in-house Earth Whisperer and sustainability superhero who ensures Stok vibes with Mother Nature on the same wavelength.
