About STOK Beer - India's Craft Beer Brand

STOK Beer is India's homegrown craft beer brand, brewed with passion by Mount Everest Breweries Limited. Born from the belief that life is best lived chilled, STOK brings you a range of premium craft beers crafted for every mood, every moment, and every vibe. Whether you're kicking back after a long day, hosting a house party, or simply soaking in life - there's a STOK for that.

Explore Our Beer Variants

STOK Lager - Our flagship lager is crisp, light, and perfectly refreshing. Brewed for those laid-back, easy-going moments when you just want to unwind and let the chill take over. STOK Lager is smooth, balanced, and utterly drinkable - the ideal companion for any occasion.

STOK Strong - For those who like their beer bold and their moments intense. STOK Strong packs a powerful punch with rich, robust flavour and a strong, satisfying finish. It's the beer for the spirited, the fearless, and the ones who live life full throttle.

STOK Wheat - Smooth, aromatic, and perfectly poised. STOK Wheat is India's premium craft wheat beer - delicately crafted for those who appreciate refinement in every sip. With a subtle complexity and a velvety texture, it's the choice for the discerning beer lover.

Brewed in India, for India

Every can of STOK Beer is a product of India's rich brewing heritage, powered by Mount Everest Breweries - one of India's most respected brewery companies. We are proud to craft beers that celebrate the Indian spirit while meeting world-class quality standards. From our brewing facility to your glass, every step of the STOK journey is guided by a singular purpose: to deliver only the finest chilled experience.
